About Us

Welcome to Rooftop Lounge Garden Bar, the premier spot in Bloemfontein for a relaxing evening with stunning views, excellent drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere. Our lounge is designed to offer an unforgettable experience, whether you're here for a casual hangout with friends or celebrating a special occasion.

Located in the heart of Ashbury, Heidedal, our venue is perfect for those who want to enjoy great music, unique cocktails, and amazing service. We pride ourselves on offering a space where you can unwind, socialize, and create lasting memories.

At Rooftop Lounge, we combine modern design with cozy charm, making it the ideal place to relax with friends or enjoy a lively night out. Come and enjoy the view, sip on a green cocktail, and discover why we are Bloemfontein's favorite rooftop bar!
